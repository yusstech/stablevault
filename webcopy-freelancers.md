## FAQ for Freelancers

**How do my international clients pay me?**
They can send money to your Relyr email, use your payment link, or pay with card. They don't need a Relyr account.

**What if my client is in a country not listed?**
We're expanding rapidly. Contact us and we'll prioritize that corridor based on demand.

**Can I invoice in one currency and receive in another?**
Yes. Invoice in USD, receive in Naira. Or hold in USD. Your choice.

**How do I withdraw to my local bank?**
One tap in the app. Choose your local bank, enter amount, done. Usually arrives same day.

**Is there a minimum withdrawal amount?**
No minimum. Withdraw $10 or $10,000. Your money, your timeline.

**What about taxes?**
Relyr provides transaction history you can export. Consult with a tax professional for your specific situation.

**Can I use this for client deposits/retainers?**
Absolutely. Many freelancers use payment links to collect 50% upfront before starting work.
