## Our Story Section

**Headline**: Why we built Relyr

**Body**:
In Africa, money doesn't just move — it travels through people's lives.

It crosses oceans when a mother sends support to her son studying abroad. It moves across borders when a small business in Lagos buys goods from China. It fuels dreams — but too often, it moves slowly, expensively, and unfairly.

We've watched Africans lose days waiting for money that should arrive in minutes. We've seen freelancers rejected by global systems that weren't built for them. We've seen mothers, traders, and small businesses find creative hacks just to survive.

Relyr was born from that frustration — not from theory.

We built Relyr because Africans deserve a better way — a faster, fairer, borderless way to move money that reflects our reality.
