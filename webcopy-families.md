## FAQ for Families

**Do they need a Relyr account to receive money?**
No. They can receive directly to their bank account or mobile money. Relyr account makes it easier, but isn't required.

**How long does it really take?**
Most transfers complete in under 5 minutes. Some corridors may take up to 24 hours, but that's rare.

**Is there a limit on how much I can send?**
Daily and monthly limits exist for regulatory compliance. Limits vary by verification level. Contact us for specific limits.

**What if I send to the wrong person?**
We have confirmation steps to prevent this. But if it happens before money is withdrawn, contact support immediately. We'll help.

**Can I send to multiple people at once?**
Yes. You can make multiple transfers in one session. Send to mum, dad, and sister all at once.

**Do I pay in my currency or theirs?**
You pay in yours. We handle the conversion. You see exactly what they'll receive before confirming.

**What if they don't have a bank account?**
Mobile money works for people without bank accounts. They just need a mobile phone.

**Can I cancel a transfer?**
Once sent, transfers happen quickly. Contact support immediately if you need to cancel. We'll try to help if money hasn't been received yet.
